Introduction to Quran Learning at iStudy

  • Comprehensive Quran Learning: Aimed at students of all ages, from beginners to advanced.
  • Focus on Tajweed: Emphasis on correct pronunciation and recitation.
  • Quran Memorization (Hifz): Structured programs for memorizing Quranic verses.
  • Understanding the Quran: Lessons on the interpretation (Tafsir) of Quranic verses.
  • Guided by Scholars: Personalized instruction from experienced Quranic scholars.
  • Flexible Learning Options: Online and on-campus classes to suit different schedules.
